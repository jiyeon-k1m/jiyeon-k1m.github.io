I am a Ph.D. student at AMILab in Convergence IT Engineering at POSTECH, advised by Tae-Hyun Oh. I received my Bachelor's degree in IT Engineering from Sookmyung Women's University. During my undergraduate, I also studied at University of Toronto as an exchange student.

My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning, specializing in understanding 3D world via 2D visual cues. Recently, my research interests are on perceiving 3D humans in the scene.
